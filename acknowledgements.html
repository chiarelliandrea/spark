Acknowledgements

This browser extension is powered by four APIs: CORE, Crosscite, OpenCitations and Unpaywall.

The extension wouldn't be possible without the significant data gathering and curation efforts of these platforms, and you are highly encouraged to cite some of their works if you use the extension for your own research.

Knoth, P. and Zdrahal., Z. (2012). CORE: three access levels to underpin open access.

Lammey, R. (2016). Using the Crossref Metadata API to explore publisher content.

Peroni, S. and Shotton, D. (2020). OpenCitations, an infrastructure organization for open scholarship.

Piwowar, H., Priem, J., Lariviere, V., Alperin, J.P., Matthias, L., Norlander, B., Farley, A., West, J. and Haustein, S. (2018). The state of OA: a large-scale analysis of the prevalence and impact of Open Access articles.
